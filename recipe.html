Tasty Momos Recipe

INGREDIENTS

Here are the essential ingredients for making delicious momos:

  • Dough:2 Cups All propose Flour
  • Water as needed
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • Fillings:500g Chicken or Banda
  • 1 small Onion,finely chopped
  • 2 GARLIC Cloves,Minced
  • 1 tablespoon Ginger paste
  • 1 Greek chilly, finely chopped
  • Salt and papper and Timmur to taste
  • 1 tabelspoon soya sauce

Directions

  1. Prepare the dough;In a bowl, mix the flour with salt. Gradually and water and knead until you form a smooth dough. cover and left it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Prapare the Fillings: In another bowl , combine the chicken aor banda, chopped onion ,garlic ,ginger and green chilli ,season with salt. pepper and soya sauce according to your taste.
  3. Divide the dough into small balls. Roll each balls into a thin circular disc.
  4. place a spoonful of Fillings in the center of each disc.
  5. Folds the edges and press to seal properly.
  6. Steam the momos in streamer for around 30 minutes.
